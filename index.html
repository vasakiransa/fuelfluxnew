How do I register a Payment personal account?

To register a Payment personal account, download our app from the App Store or Google Play. Open the app and click on 'Sign Up.' Enter your personal details, such as name, email, and phone number. Once you’ve created your account, you can add your delivery location, choose the type of fuel you need, and place your order. A confirmation will be sent to your email, and our delivery team will contact you shortly.

What if I need to cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order directly through the app. Navigate to your order history, select the order you wish to change, and choose the 'Edit' or 'Cancel' option. If the order has already been dispatched, please contact our support team immediately for assistance.

How can I provide feedback on my experience?

We value your feedback! You can provide feedback through the app by selecting 'Feedback' in the menu or contacting our support team directly via email or phone. Your feedback helps us improve our service and customer experience.

What types of fuel can I order?

Our platform supports various fuel types, including gasoline, diesel, and compressed natural gas (CNG). When placing an order, you can select the fuel type that best suits your needs.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept several payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and popular digital wallets like PayPal and Google Pay. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.
